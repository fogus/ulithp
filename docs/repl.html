Extending μLithp with a Read-Eval-Print-Loop (REPL)

Again my friend Russ Olsen took some time to play with μLithp and wrote a little REPL, shown below:

REPL

require 'lithp'
require 'reader'

lisp = Lisp.new

print "> "
while not $stdin.eof?
  line = readline
  s_expression = Reader.new(line).read
  p lisp.eval(s_expression)
  print "> "
end

Usage

From your shell of choice, execute the following: 

> rvm use ruby-1.9.3
> ruby -I . repl.rb

This will put you in an interactive shell allowing a more traditional Lisp feel: 

(car (quote (1 2 3)))
;;=> 1

(cdr (quote (1 2 3)))
;;=> [2, 3]

(label second (quote (lambda (x) (car (cdr x))))) 
(second (quote (1 2 3)))
;;=> 2
